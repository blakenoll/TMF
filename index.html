The Master’s Fellowship is an association of churches and church leaders affiliated with the ministries of John MacArthur. Our desire is to network like-minded pastors and churches who are faithful to the inerrant truth of biblical doctrine. The purpose of this network is to facilitate the mission of The Master’s University and Seminary in training up the next generation of pastors, teachers and faithful church members to know and serve the Lord Jesus Christ.

I AM BOUND AND COMPELLED BY LOVE FOR THE TRUTH, WRITTEN AND INCARNATE. FOR ALL THOSE WHO SHARE THAT BLESSED BONDAGE AND PASSION I CALL YOU TO COME TOGETHER WITH ME IN THE MASTER’S FELLOWSHIP.

  • Recommended resources from John MacArthur and various faculty members
  • Invitations to exclusive online member-only events like live Q&A's and interactive webcasts
  • 10% off online coursework through The Master's University Online for active church members excludes graduate studies, traditional students, and dual enrollment students
  • 33% off courses offered through The Master's Seminary Institute for Church Leadership for active church members
  • 10% off Hymns of Grace for active church members
  • Receive direct correspondence including artwork, posters, bulletin announcements, etc. regarding events of interest to your local congregation for both youth and adults

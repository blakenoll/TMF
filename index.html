The Master's Fellowship

The Master’s Fellowship is an association of churches and church leaders affiliated with the ministries of John MacArthur. Our desire is to network like-minded pastors and solid churches who are faithful to the inerrant truth of biblical doctrine. The purpose of this network is to facilitate the mission of The Master’s University and Seminary in training up the next generation of pastors, teachers and faithful church members to know and serve the Lord Jesus Christ.

Through this a liated network, we intend to not only continue our ministry of partnering with local churches to train pastors and bright young people, but to also reinvest back into your local church. Members of this network will receive a number of bene ts from The Master’s University and Seminary.

--- title: TMF Nebraska - About layout: single --- About TMF Nebraska We are a network of pastors in Nebraska committed to the expository proclamation of God's Word. This fellowship is bound together by a firm belief in the inerrancy and sufficiency of Scripture. Through the mutual support of pastoral fellowship, our mission is to strengthen the leadership of like-minded local churches in Nebraska. Our vision for TMFNE is to forge an online community and to host in-person gatherings for encouragement, accountability, and equipping; so that each of us may fulfill the shepherding ministry entrusted to us by Christ, our Savior and Master.
